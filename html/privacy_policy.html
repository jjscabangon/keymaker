Privacy Policy of Key Maker

At Key Maker, we value your privacy and want you to feel confident about how we handle your information. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Before sharing any personal details, please review this policy. By using Key Maker, you agree that we can collect, use, and share your information as described below.

What Information We Collect

When you create an account, post a job, or apply for a job, we may collect:

  1. Personal Information: Details like your name, email, phone number, and location..
  2. Profile Information: Job seekers may provide information like work experience, education, and skills, while employers may provide company details, such as contact information and descriptions.
  3. Identification Documents:For verification purposes, job seekers may need to provide documents like a police clearance, and employers may need to submit a business permit to confirm their legitimacy.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, run, and improve our website.
  • Help match job seekers and employers.
  • Connect job seekers and employers for job opportunities.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your information to third parties. Your information is only shared in these cases:

  • With Other Users: Your profile and some personal details may be visible to other users (job seekers and employers) to help with job matching.
  • With Our Team:Our team (developers and admins) may access your information to improve and manage the website. We do not share sensitive details outside of Key Maker without your permission.
